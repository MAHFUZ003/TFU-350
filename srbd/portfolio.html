About Me

I am a passionate Machine Learning researcher with a B.Sc. in Computer Science and Engineering from Feni University. My research interests lie at the intersection of Computer Vision and Large Language Models, with a focus on developing practical solutions to complex problems.

Currently working as a Jr. Software Engineer at Agamilabs, I balance industry experience with academic research. My undergraduate thesis on social gathering classification using YOLOv8 achieved 86% accuracy, and I'm currently exploring hallucination analysis in LLMs and image reconstruction using deep learning.

I am seeking funded MSc opportunities in Malaysia to further my research in AI and contribute to the academic community.
